The Solution

Meet DR. SparkPlay.

DR. SparkPlay is a web platform that runs the entire day of an early learning centre — sign-in, observations, programming, daily forms, compliance, and family communication — in one place, with AI doing the heavy lifting on the writing.

In other words: a very organised assistant director who never calls in sick, has memorised the regulations of every state and territory, and lives in your browser.

🌞

Run the day

Kiosk sign-in, roll call, day plans, activities, observations, handover — the daily rhythm of a centre, minus the paper cuts.

🛡️

Stay compliant

Ratios, medication logs, incident reports, NQS self-assessment, QIP — compliance built into the workflow, not stapled on after.

💬

Keep families close

Daily diaries, messages, community wall, broadcasts in 15 languages, invoices — parents in the loop, not in the dark.

Demo · How It Actually Works

A day with DR. SparkPlay

7:30am
Families arrive. Kiosk sign-in with a wellbeing check-in — no clipboard, no pen on a string.
9:00am
Roll call. Live ratio check against your state's rules. Allergy and EpiPen badges right on the room board — no memory tests with children's lives.
10:15am
An educator snaps an observation. One save and it's shared with the parent, the child's interests are updated, and a linked activity lands in next week's program. Written once. Typed zero additional times.
12:30pm
Medication given and logged — dose, route, authorisation, witness. Regulation 93 compliant without anyone opening the regulations.
2:00pm
Planning time. The AI drafts an activity from the children's actual current interests, mapped to EYLF outcomes. The educator tweaks and approves. Ten minutes, not ninety.
5:45pm
Shift handover typed and acknowledged by the incoming educator. The sacred paper handover book retires with full honours.
6:01pm
Everyone goes home. On time. Revolutionary, we know.

5
The AI Assistant

It does the writing.
You do the teaching.

✨

Activity generator

Turns the children's real, current interests into EYLF-mapped activity plans. "Dinosaurs and puddles" in, learning outcomes out.

📄

Document import & review

Upload the policy you wrote in 2019. Get a quality score, the gaps against NQS/EYLF standards, and specific fixes. The file isn't stored — read, reviewed, discarded.

🎒

Transition statements

Strengths-based, privacy-safe school transition drafts. The educator edits and finalises — the AI just does the blank-page part.

🌏

Broadcasts in 15 languages

Write the newsletter once; every family reads it in their own language. No more "hope Google Translate got that right."

🪞

Guided reflections

Generates genuine reflection prompts (Gibbs & Driscoll frameworks) so professional development is actual reflection, not creative box-ticking.

🖼️

Posters & Auslan

A poster and flier maker with built-in image search, plus an Auslan dictionary linked to real Signbank videos.

Every AI output is a draft. Educators review, edit, and approve — the human stays the author.

Compliance

Assessor visits: from cold sweat
to mild, confident glow.

Compliance in DR. SparkPlay isn't a module you visit. It's baked into the tools educators already use every day.

Jurisdiction-aware ratios for all 9 Australian states & territories, checked live at roll call
Mandatory reporting guidance — the correct legislation for your state, right on the incident report
NQS self-assessment — all 7 quality areas and 15 standards, with ratings and evidence notes
QIP with regular check-ins, so quality improvement is a habit, not a pre-assessment panic
Medication log built to Regulation 93 — dose, route, authorisation, witness
Visitor log with WWCC and ID checks (NQS Quality Area 2)
Staff compliance tracker — WWCC, First Aid, Anaphylaxis, with expiry alerts before they lapse
Risk assessments & safety checks — templated, logged, findable in seconds
